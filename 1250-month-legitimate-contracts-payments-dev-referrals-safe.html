The Foundation: A Legitimate Referral Agreement

  • Commission Rate: Clearly states the 10% commission on the total project value for successful referrals.

  • Tracking & Transparency: How referrals are tracked and how you can monitor their progress.

  • Term & Termination: The duration of the agreement and conditions for ending the partnership.
