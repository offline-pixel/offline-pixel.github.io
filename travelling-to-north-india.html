Frequently Asked Questions for Travellers in Delhi

Q: What’s the best way for foreign travellers in Delhi to get from IGI Airport to Connaught Place?

A: The Delhi Airport Metro Express is fast, affordable (~₹60–₹120), and takes ~20 minutes. Alternatively, take app‑based taxis like Ola/Uber, which are convenient for solo travellers in Delhi.

Q: Where can travellers in Delhi find authentic street food?

A: Try Old Delhi (Chandni Chowk), Khan Market, and Lajpat Nagar. Don’t miss chaat, golgappas, parathas, and kebabs. These areas are popular with all travellers in New Delhi.

Q: Is New Delhi safe for solo female travellers?

A: Yes, with proper precautions. For solo female travellers in Delhi, use women‑only metros after dark, avoid isolated areas, and use app‑based taxis with live tracking for enhanced safety. Many solo female travellers visit New Delhi safely.

Q: How do foreign travellers in Delhi stay connected—SIM, eSIM, Wi‑Fi—in India?

A: Foreign travellers in New Delhi can buy a prepaid SIM (Airtel/Jio/Vodafone) at the airport. eSIMs are available via international providers. Most hotels and cafés offer free Wi‑Fi, which is convenient for all travellers.

Q: What are the best cultural spots for solo travellers in Delhi?

A: Solo travellers in Delhi can explore historical sites like Humayun's Tomb and Qutub Minar, or vibrant markets like Dilli Haat and Hauz Khas Village. Many hostels also organize walking tours and social events for meeting other solo travellers in New Delhi.
