Why Tailor Your Approach by Client Size?

  • Different Needs & Budgets: Small businesses have different problems and budgets than mid-sized companies or large enterprises.

  • Targeted Communication: Your message and the way you approach a lead should resonate with their specific context.
  • Optimized Effort: Direct your time and energy to the types of clients that align with your income goals.

Tailoring Your Approach:

1. Small Businesses (Target: $500-$1000/Month Commissions)

Who they are: Local shops, independent professionals (consultants, real estate agents), startups, small service providers (plumbers, electricians, salons).

  • Typical Needs: Basic professional website, mobile-friendly redesign, simple e-commerce setup, online booking system, lead generation website.
  • How to Find:
    • Local Observation: Drive around your neighborhood, check local business directories, search Google Maps.
    • Personal Network: Friends, family, acquaintances who own or work at small businesses.
    • Local Business Groups: Chamber of Commerce, local networking events (online or in-person).
  • Your Approach:
    • Focus on immediate pain points: "Is your website mobile-friendly?", "Are you getting enough online leads?", "Could an online booking system save you time?"
    • Emphasize speed & simplicity: They want quick, effective solutions without complexity.
    • Warm Introductions: Personal, direct introductions are highly effective due to existing trust.
  • Commission Potential: Projects typically range from $5,000-$10,000, yielding $500-$1,000 per referral. These are your "easy wins" for consistent foundational income.

2. Mid-Sized Businesses (Target: $1000-$2500/Month Commissions)

Who they are: Regional companies, growing e-commerce brands, established service firms, companies with 20-200 employees.

  • Typical Needs: Custom web applications, CRM integrations, robust e-commerce platforms, internal tools for efficiency, complex website redesigns with advanced features, scalable digital infrastructure.
  • How to Find:
    • LinkedIn Prospecting: Target specific job titles (Marketing Manager, Operations Director, Department Head) in mid-sized companies.
    • Industry Associations: Join relevant industry groups where these businesses network.
    • Complementary Partners: Connect with business consultants, marketing agencies, or IT service providers who serve mid-sized clients.
  • Your Approach:
    • Focus on efficiency & growth: "How can digital solutions help you scale?", "Are your current systems hindering growth?", "How can we streamline your operations?"
    • Highlight ROI: They are more likely to understand the return on investment for a larger digital project.
    • Professional, Value-Driven Introductions: Provide more context to Offline Pixel Computers about their specific challenges.
  • Commission Potential: Projects typically range from $10,000-$25,000, yielding $1,000-$2,500 per referral. These are your consistent, higher-value opportunities.

3. Large Enterprises (Target: $2500-$5000+/Month Commissions)

Who they are: Corporations, large non-profits, national brands, companies with 200+ employees.

  • Typical Needs: Enterprise-level custom software, complex system integrations (ERP, CRM), large-scale SaaS development, digital transformation initiatives, secure data management platforms, multi-faceted digital ecosystems.
  • How to Find:
    • Executive-Level Networking: Connect with CIOs, CTOs, VPs of Digital Transformation, or Heads of Innovation.
    • Industry Conferences: Attend major national or international events (virtual or in-person).
    • Strategic Partnerships: Focus on high-level consultants, legal firms, or financial advisors who serve large enterprises.
    • News & Funding: Monitor news for large companies announcing digital initiatives, new funding, or acquisitions.
  • Your Approach:
    • Focus on strategic impact & risk mitigation: "How can digital solutions drive competitive advantage?", "How can we ensure data security and compliance?", "How can we integrate disparate systems for seamless operations?"
    • Be patient: Sales cycles are longer, involve more stakeholders, and require more detailed qualification.
    • Detailed Briefing: Provide comprehensive information to Offline Pixel Computers to ensure our team is fully prepared for the complex sales process.
  • Commission Potential: Projects typically range from $25,000 to $100,000+, yielding $2,500-$10,000+ per referral. These are your "whale" projects that can significantly boost your income.
