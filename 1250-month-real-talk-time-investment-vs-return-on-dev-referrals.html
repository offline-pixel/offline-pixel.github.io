Breaking Down Your Time Investment:

Phase 2: Lead Identification & Qualification (Ongoing, Low-to-Moderate Effort)

This is your primary ongoing activity. It's about being observant and making smart connections.

Total Ongoing Time: Approximately 1-3 hours per week.

Phase 3: Introduction & Handoff (Minimal Effort)

This is the critical moment, but it's remarkably quick.

  • Making the Warm Intro: Sending a 3-way email or making a brief 3-way call to connect your lead with Offline Pixel Computers. (Approx. 5-10 minutes per introduction)
  • Providing Context: Briefly briefing our team on the lead's needs and background. (Approx. 5 minutes per introduction)

Total Time per Introduction: Approximately 10-15 minutes.

Phase 4: Follow-up & Nurturing (Light, Periodic Effort)

Keeping tabs on your referrals and nurturing your network.

  • Checking In: A quick, non-intrusive follow-up with both the client and our team to ensure the connection was made and things are progressing. (Approx. 5 minutes per lead, periodically)
  • Network Nurturing: Staying connected with your network, offering value, and being a helpful resource. This builds long-term trust. (Ongoing, integrated into your routine)

Total Ongoing Time: Approximately 30-60 minutes per week.
