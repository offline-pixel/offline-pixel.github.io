The Simple Math: 10% Commission Model

Our core offering is a 10% commission on the total project value once a deal is closed and payments are made. To figure out how to earn $500, we can use a basic formula:

$$ \text{Desired Commission} = \text{Project Value} \times \text{Commission Rate} $$

To find the project value needed for a $500 commission:

$$ \text{Project Value} = \frac{\text{Desired Commission}}{\text{Commission Rate}} $$

Plugging in our numbers:

$$ \text{Project Value} = \frac{\$500}{0.10} $$

$$ \text{Project Value} = \$5,000 $$

This means for every $5,000worth of web or software development projects you refer that successfully close, you earn $500.
