A well-structured referral partnership defines roles, streamlines processes, and ensures clear compensation. Here’s how ours works to help you aim for $750+ monthly:

  2. The Critical Handoff: Facilitating the Initial Call.

    This is where the structure truly helps. Instead of you explaining complex technical details or negotiating, your role is to facilitate an introduction and set up a brief, initial consultation callbetween the potential client and our team.   3. Our Role: The Expert Developer & Closer.

    • Conduct in-depth consultations to understand the client's needs.

    • Process all payments and financial aspects of the project.

    This division means you never get bogged down in the complex, time-consuming aspects of web development or sales.

  4. The Clear Compensation: Your 10% Commission.
