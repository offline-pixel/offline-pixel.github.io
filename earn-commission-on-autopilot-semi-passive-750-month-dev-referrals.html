Understanding "Semi-Passive" in Dev Referrals:

Unlike a true "passive" income where money flows without any active involvement (e.g., dividends from stocks), "semi-passive" income means your significant initial efforts create momentum that then requires only periodic, lighter maintenance to continue generating revenue. For web development referrals, this looks like:

  • Initial Effort: Actively seeking out and making warm introductions for businesses needing digital solutions. This requires a focused effort initially.
  • Ongoing, Lower Effort: Once connections are made and clients are satisfied, referrals often come through organic word-of-mouth, repeat business from existing clients, or your network actively thinking of you for new needs.
