How to Practice Precision in Your Referrals:

  1. Deep Listen/Observe: During conversations or research, go beyond surface-level needs. Ask "why?" and "what problem does this solve?" Listen for frustrations, inefficiencies, or ambitious growth plans.
  2. Industry Focus: Pick one or two industries you understand well or have connections in. Learn their common pain points and specialized software needs.
  3. Research Specific Competitors: Look at what successful businesses in a niche are using. If a competitor has a custom app or feature, that suggests a need for similar solutions.
  4. Ask Targeted Questions: Instead of "Do you need a website?", ask "How do you manage your customer bookings?" or "What's the biggest bottleneck in your supply chain?"
  5. Educate Yourself (Broadly): Understand the *types* of solutions custom software can provide, even if you don't know the technical details. This helps you recognize the "specific dev work" opportunity.
